Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GameNest World ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit gamenestworld.com (the "Site") and play our games.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not require account registration to play games. If you contact us by email, we collect whatever information you choose to include in that message (name, email address, message content).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URL, pages viewed, and timestamps. This is collected through standard server logs and analytics tools.

1.3 Local Game Data

Your game scores, XP, achievements, and streak data are stored locally in your browser (using localStorage) and are not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly opt into a leaderboard feature. Clearing your browser data will erase this local progress.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising purposes. See our Cookie Policy for full details on what cookies we use and how to control them.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Google Analytics collects information such as pages visited, time on site, and general geographic location (derived from IP address). You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

5. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site and our games
  • To analyze usage trends and site performance
  • To display relevant advertising
  • To respond to support requests and inquiries
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited technical data with service providers who help us operate the Site (analytics providers, advertising networks, hosting providers), each of whom is bound by their own privacy obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

GameNest World is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@gamenestworld.com and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to certain processing (including for advertising purposes). To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@gamenestworld.com.

9. Data Retention

Server logs are typically retained for a limited period for security and diagnostic purposes. Locally stored game data remains on your device until you clear your browser storage.

10. International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at privacy@gamenestworld.com or visit our Contact page.
