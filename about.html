Our Features

Progressive Difficulty: Every game features up to 50 levels, starting easy and scaling to expert. Beginners can enjoy casual play while seasoned gamers can push their limits.

XP & Achievements: Earn experience points for every game you play and win. Level up your profile and unlock achievements that track your progress across all games.

High Score System: Enter your name once and your scores are saved permanently in your browser. Compete against your own personal bests.

Daily Challenges: A new featured game every day with bonus XP rewards. Come back daily to maintain your streak and earn extra points.

No Account Required: We respect your privacy. Everything is stored locally in your browser — we don't collect personal information.
