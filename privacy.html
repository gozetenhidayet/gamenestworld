Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Overview

GameNest World ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. Short version: we collect very little data, and we never sell it.

Information We Store Locally

GameNest World stores the following data in your browser's local storage — this data never leaves your device:

  • Your chosen player name (for high score display)
  • Your XP points and level progress
  • High scores per game
  • Favorite games list
  • Sound preference (on/off)
  • Daily challenge completion status

You can clear all this data anytime by clearing your browser's local storage or site data.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how players use our site. This includes anonymous data like page views, session duration, and which games are most popular. Google Analytics uses cookies. We do not have access to personally identifiable information through this service.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising

GameNest World may display advertisements through Google AdSense to support free access to all games. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences through Google Ad Settings.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for basic site functionality, and Google Analytics/AdSense cookies as described above. We do not use any tracking cookies ourselves.

Children's Privacy

GameNest World is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our games do not require account creation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will note the date of the last update at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our website at gamenestworld.com.
