Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

In short: you don't need an account to play any game on GameNest World, we don't sell your personal data, and the only information collected comes from standard analytics and advertising services described below.

1. Information we collect

GameNest World does not require registration, login, or the submission of personal information such as your name or email address to use the site or play any game. Gameplay data — including your XP, streaks, achievements and preferred language — is stored locally in your browser (via localStorage) and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site — for example, which games are popular and which pages are visited — so we can improve the experience. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as approximate location, device type, and on-site behavior. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can learn more or opt out via Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

GameNest World displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or via www.aboutads.info if you are outside the EU.

4. Cookies

Cookies used on this site fall into two categories: those set by Google Analytics (to measure usage) and those set by Google AdSense (to serve and measure ads). We do not use cookies to store any personally identifiable information ourselves. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings, though some site features — such as saved language preference — may stop working as expected.

5. Children's privacy

GameNest World is a general-audience site and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us using the details below so we can address it.

6. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any data held about you by our third-party providers (Google Analytics, Google AdSense), and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of data for targeted advertising. Because we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, these requests are best directed to Google directly using the links in sections 2 and 3, or to us so we can assist.

7. Third-party links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their own privacy policies.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled? Reach out via our Contact page.
