Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

GameNest World is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage: Your XP, achievements, streaks and preferences are stored only in your browser's local storage — never on our servers.
  • No Account Required: We never ask for your name, email or personal information.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns (page views, device types). This is anonymous.

2. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for analytics purposes only. Advertising cookies may be set by Google AdSense when ads are displayed.

3. Advertising

GameNest World uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site contains affiliate links to third-party sites (Lumosity, Chess.com, Coursera, Amazon). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

GameNest World is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, there is no risk of server-side data breaches affecting your personal information.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: hello@gamenestworld.com
